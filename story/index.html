Our Story

Once in a while, right in the middle of an ordinary life, love gives us a fairy tale.

07 Nov 2021

First Meet

It is an arranged marriage, we decided to meet at the temple. We met at the Murugan temple in Virudhunagar. It was love at first sight and we exchanged a few words. It is a lifetime moment to cherish. If we could only control the time, We would go back to the moment when we both first met, not to change anything, but to experience it all over again!

14 Nov 2021

Engagement

Exactly one week later it was decided to have the engagement. During this one-week break, we didn't get a chance to meet or talk. Finally we met on the stage. We exchanged ring and gifts. We took photos together. Those moments are unforgettable in life. The day our love fairy tale started

14 Feb 2022

Our Wedding

The best part of life is spending each and every day with the fiance, Long phone calls and late-night messages, shopping for your wedding dress, preparing your wedding vows with the excitement and eagerness of waiting for the big day!
